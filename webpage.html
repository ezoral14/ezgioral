About Me

Ezgi Oral is a PhD Research Scholar at the Department of Economics at NHH and is affiliated with the Choice Lab under FAIR. She earned her BA degree in Economics with a double major in Psychology from Koc University and holds an MA in Economics from the same institution. Her current research interests revolve around examining how social factors influence fairness considerations in childhood, focusing on psychological mechanisms and social identity development.

Ongoing Projects

Ezgi is involved in several research projects focusing on social factors and fairness considerations in childhood. Her projects explore the development of social identity and psychological mechanisms in children, as well as large-scale interventions to enhance non-cognitive skills in educational environments.
